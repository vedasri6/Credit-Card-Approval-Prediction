About Aegis Decision Platform

Aegis is an advanced machine learning architecture engineered for predictive modeling and risk assessment in retail consumer credit operations. Designed as a complete end-to-end engineering project, the platform implements a pipeline of cleaning, feature derivation, and benchmarking of eleven different classifiers to optimize classification metrics.

System Architecture Workflow
1
Synthesizer & Ingestion

Generates or ingests CSV tables with multi-variable demographics and bureau flags.

2
Imputation & Scaling

Resolves missing fields with median/mode rules, caps numerical outliers, and standardizes scales.

3
Feature Engineering

Derives credit scoring metrics including Debt-to-Income and leverage coefficients.

4
Ensemble soft voting

Compares models (Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM) and selects the best performer for serialization.
